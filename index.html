Jyh-Jing Hwang

jyh @ seas . upenn . edu

Jyh-Jing Hwang is a Ph.D. student in computer science advised by Prof. Stella Yu at UC Berkeley / ICSI and Prof. Jianbo Shi at University of Pennsylvania. He obtained his B.S. and M.S. degrees from National Taiwan University and has worked with Dr. Tyng-Luh Liu as a student researcher at Academia Sinica. Jyh-Jing is interested in using machine learning and computer vision to understand general image scenes, especially related to human action.
