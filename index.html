        Hi! I am a Ph.D. student in Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Prof. Stella Yu at UC Berkeley / ICSI and Prof. Jianbo Shi. I am very fortunate to have the opportunities to intern at Facebook AI Research and Google Research during my study.
        Before coming to the U.S., I obtained the B.S. and M.S. degrees in EE, advised by Prof. Liang-Gee Chen, from National Taiwan University and worked with Dr. Tyng-Luh Liu as a research assistant at Academia Sinica.
        My research interests are broadly in computer vision and machine learning. Particularly, I am interested in understanding general image/video structures and scenes.
