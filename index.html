Jyh-Jing Hwang

jyh @ seas . upenn . edu

Jyh-Jing Hwang is a Ph.D. student in Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania advised by Prof. Stella Yu at UC Berkeley / ICSI and Prof. Jianbo Shi. He obtained his B.S. and M.S. degrees in EE from National Taiwan University and has worked with Dr. Tyng-Luh Liu as a student researcher at Academia Sinica. Jyh-Jing is interested in using machine learning and computer vision to understand general image structures and scenes.
